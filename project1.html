Usability test script



Thank you for agreeing to perform this usability test. Note that we are testing how easy it is to use the “lab book” application. This website which is a demo version of an android watch application describes the activities and experiences of students as they develop a user interface and the corresponding application. We are not testing you. We are interested in discovering what problems you encounter while using this website. Don’t worry about making mistakes. They will help us improve the “lab book” application.
Please set your browser to https://cs560pdx.github.io/example.html
TASK Spent Time
Choose Sleeping part, type in the amount of sleeping hours for today, click DONE.
Choose Sleeping part, modify the amount of sleeping hours for today, click DONE.
Choose Water part, type in the number of glasses of water, click ADD.
Choose Activity part, choose an activity from the list, type in the amount of time spending for that activity, click ADD.
Choose Activity part, click Create New Activity button, type in the name of the activity and calories per hour for that activity, click CREATE ACTIVITY.

Questions Answers
On a scale from 1 to 5 with 1 representing bad and 5 representing great, how easy were the application to use?
On a scale from 1 to 5 with 1 representing bad and 5 representing great, how did you like the “look and feel” of the example?
What did you think should be added to the example website?
What did you like best about the application?

Thank you for providing your time and insight to help us improve this application.

Summerize

Users mostly like the simplicity of example application and how it was easy to use. The other thing that was interesting to them was the appearance. Some of them prefered different format for representing hours in sleeping hour part.

I realized that every individual has his or her own angle of seeing the project which can help us see the projects in different perspectives. They came up with subtle different ideas for a specific problem which teach us how it is important to have idea of different people and learn from them.
